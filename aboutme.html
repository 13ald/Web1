About Me - Shane Finlayson

I am a Mixologist and hardware specialist currently employed by the largest vape company in the uk VPZ UK. I am excited to show you all of the work that I do and teach you the secrets of mixing your own e-liquid. I have been one of two in house mixologists for VPZ for a over a year now and I have contributed to many of the ranges they have brought out in recent times. Some of my other work includes doing 60% of the newest range for PACHA MAMA.

A lot of my time goes into testing out the latest concetrates from big name brands as well as some lesser known ones in the industry. This has given me an extensive amount of knowledge of flavours and combination. When it comes to making recipes it has become second nature to me in knowing what will work best to make X.
